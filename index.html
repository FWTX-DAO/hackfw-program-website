Our Mission

HackFW creates a sustainable innovation flywheel for Fort Worth by exposing youth and workforce to hands-on technical product development in data and software engineering.

Through real-world challenges and industry mentorship, we're building a pipeline that keeps technical talent local while establishing Fort Worth as an emerging tech hub.

We'll lead with the Fort Worth DAO as an open-source, Web3 civic-minded digital sovereignty initiative, connecting winning builders with problem sponsors to accelerate product-market fit and real-world deployment.
